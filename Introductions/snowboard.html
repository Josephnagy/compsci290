Arapahoe Basin, CO

December 2018

This was my first time snowboarding! It was a very spontaneous trip that my friends and I planned two weeks before winter break. Despite throwing it together at the last minute, everything worked out great and we had an awesome time. We even got a blizzard while we were there!

Stratton, VT

December 2019

This time I went snowboarding with my uncle and two cousins. Snowboarding in Stratton was the first time I really started to feel confidence going down the mountain. Even though it was pretty icy towards the end of the trip, I really enjoyed myself.

Breckenridge, CO

March 2020

This trip was the last time I was out west. My friends and I went on this trip right after spring break last year. This trip was extremely memorable for me because I had a fantastic time, but also because this was right before we went into the spring COVID-19 lockdown.

Mountain Creek, NJ

January 2021

This was the last time I went snowboarding! I took my brother to a very small mountain in Vernon NJ called Mountain Creek. It was his first time snowboarding, but after only a few runs on the bunny hill, he was ready to try out the greens! Even though most of the runs were closed due to COVID-19 restrictions we had a great time getting out of the house for a few days.
